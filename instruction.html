Instructions!

1. Touch or swipe candies to move them around.
2. Put three of the same color together.
3. Do what the game tells you to do, like getting a certain score.
4. Always keep an eye on Moves Left and Target
5. You are always welcome to play game again
6. You will get 10 points if you'll crush three candies.
7. You can only swap if three same candies are present.
